AI-Driven ERP Systems for Small & Medium Enterprises

Research conducted under the International STEM Research Program, Incognito Blueprints

Abstract

This research explores the implementation and performance of AI-powered ERP systems tailored for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). It focuses on enhancing scalability, security, and operational efficiency in open-source ERP platforms.

  • Adaptive customer journey mapping
  • Real-time inventory and demand forecasting
  • Security enhancement for open-source ERP platforms

Improved operational efficiency by 25%

Reduced decision-making latency by 30%

Enhanced project success metrics by 20%

The system was benchmarked using datasets from SMEs in India, the United States, and Spain to evaluate global scalability.
